## Negotiation Tips (Philippine context)

### Common objections and responses

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| **"That's too expensive"** | "Let me show you the scope vs alternatives. A US agency would charge 3-4x this." |
| **"Can we do it cheaper?"** | "Yes, we can reduce scope. Which features should we remove for MVP?" |
| **"My friend can do it for ₱500K"** | "That's a different quality level. Ask about their Supabase/Stripe integration experience." |
| **"Can we pay after launch?"** | "I can offer milestone payments, but I can't fund the entire project." |

### When to walk away

- ❌ Client wants full app for <₱500K (PHP) or <$15K (international)
- ❌ They want it in 4-6 weeks ("just simple app")
- ❌ No clear budget or decision-maker
- ❌ Asking for spec work before contract

## Red Flags in Philippine Market

Be cautious of:
- 🚩 Clients asking for "Facebook clone" budgets (₱300K for everything)
- 🚩 "We'll pay you in equity" (unless you believe in the product + have cash runway)
- 🚩 "My cousin/nephew can code, just need to make it work"
- 🚩 Paying only after app approval (Apple review can take weeks)
- 🚩 No contract, just "trust"
